A bit about me

I take the time to actually sit and talk with you

Most people who come to see me have already had at least one difficult conversation before they get to me. A diagnosis. A scan result. Being told there are options but not being told which one makes sense. By the time they sit down in my clinic, the last thing they need is another rushed appointment.

So I don't do that. I ask questions, I listen to the answers, and then I give my honest opinion — not a menu of options with a shrug, but a clear view on what I think should happen and why. If I think someone else would serve you better, I'll say so.
